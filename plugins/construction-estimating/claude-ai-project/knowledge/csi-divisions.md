# CSI MasterFormat Divisions & Scope-Gap Guide

*(facts as-of: 2026-07 — re-verify dated items: code editions, tax rates, statutes, $/SF bands)*

Organize every takeoff, scope, and estimate by **CSI MasterFormat division** so nothing
is missed and trades reconcile cleanly. Below: the divisions, typical bid items, and the
**scope gaps** that most often cause double-counts or holes between trades.

## Scope-gap checklist — the items trades fight over (assign each to exactly one party)

- **Blocking / backing / in-wall support** for grab bars, TVs, casework, equipment (06 vs. the trade).
- **Concrete housekeeping / equipment pads** (03 vs. MEP).
- **Roof curbs, blocking, pitch pans, flashing** at penetrations (07 vs. 22/23).
- **Flashing & sealants** at openings — head/jamb/sill (07 vs. 08).
- **Firestopping** at rated-wall penetrations (07 vs. each MEP trade).
- **Access panels / access doors** (08/09 vs. MEP).
- **Louvers** — wall (08) vs. mechanical (23).
- **Painting** of HM frames, exposed structure, MEP (09 vs. trade — confirm who primes/finishes).
- **Final connections** of owner-furnished equipment (OFCI) — power/plumbing/anchorage.
- **Excavation/backfill for footings** (31) vs. **for utilities** (33) vs. **MEP under-slab** (22/26).
- **Concrete encasement / thrust blocks** for utilities (33 vs. 03).
- **Temporary power, water, hoisting, cranes, dumpsters, layout** (Div 01 GC vs. trades).
- **Patching & repair** after MEP rough-in / cutting (who restores?).
- **Cutting & coring** of existing/new concrete and masonry.
- **Sleeves & embeds** set in concrete/masonry (set by trade, furnished by trade?).
- **Controls integration / line-voltage to low-voltage** boundary (23 vs. 26 vs. 25/28).

For each scope item, classify it: **By us / By others / By owner / Excluded / Allowance /
Alternate / Unit price**. Carry the same classification consistently through the scope
narrative AND the estimate so they never disagree.
