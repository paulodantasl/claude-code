# RFP Email Template

Fill in the placeholders and send. All placeholders are required except
`{{DEADLINE_NOTES}}`.

## Envelope

- **From**: your bidding inbox (e.g. `bidding@yourdomain.com`)
- **To**: your bidding inbox (same address — used as anchor for replies)
- **BCC**: one or more vendor email addresses. Vendors do not see each other.
- **Reply-To**: your bidding inbox
- **Subject**: `RFP — {{SCOPE}} — {{SITE_ADDRESS}} — due {{DEADLINE_DATE}}`

## Body

```
Hello,

We are requesting a bid for {{SCOPE}} on the project below.

Site: {{SITE_ADDRESS}}
Deadline for bids: {{DEADLINE_DATE}} {{DEADLINE_NOTES}}

Scope of work:
{{SCOPE_OF_WORK}}

Plans: {{PLANS_URL}}

Please include in your bid:
  - Line-item pricing by material / labor / equipment
  - Lead time from PO to mobilization
  - Payment terms
  - Copies of your W-9 and current COI (if we don't already have them)

Reply to this email with your bid or with any questions. Feel free to
walk the site — we can meet by appointment.

Thanks,
{{SENDER_NAME}}
{{COMPANY}}
{{PHONE}}
```

## Do not include

- Our estimated budget, cost range, allowance, or previous bid numbers.
- The names or emails of other vendors we are requesting from.
- Any content that would let one vendor infer we are shopping their bid.
